Four Days in Music City

01

Yeehaw, We're Here

Thursday, June 4  ·  Arrive by 3 PM

🌄
3:00 PM

Arrive & Check In

Roll up to our Nashville home base, drop your bags, and get acquainted with the crew. Welcome drinks await — the weekend officially begins.

5:00 PM

Bridal Games 🎁

Time to play! We kick off the weekend with classic bachelorette games — "How Well Do You Know the Bride," He Said She Said, and more. Prizes, laughs, and maybe a little friendly competition to get everyone warmed up.

7:00 PM

Cook Dinner Together

We cook for ourselves tonight — a homemade meal, good wine, and even better company. The perfect low-key way to ease into the weekend before things really get going.

Matching PJ Night 🌛

Slip into your matching sets and settle in for a cozy night together. This is the calm before the storm — good snacks, good company, and getting hyped for the weekend ahead.

Sun, Sip & Shop

Friday, June 5

☀️
10:00 AM

Pool & Tanning Day

Sunscreen on, drinks poured. Spend the morning lounging poolside, soaking up the Tennessee sun. Full glam not required — just a good attitude and your best swimsuit.

Included with rental
12:00 PM

Backyard Grill Lunch

We fire up the grill right outside. Burgers, sides, cold drinks — a proper southern cookout without leaving the backyard. We'll also be stocking up on snacks, drinks, and decorations for the house to set the mood all weekend. A more detailed group budget breakdown will be sent separately.

3:00 PM

Shopping + Bar Taco 🌮

We head out to explore Nashville's best boutiques and local shops — the kind of afternoon where you find something you didn't know you needed. We'll be shopping around the Bar Taco area, so expect great stores, great bites, and great vibes all in one neighborhood.

Bar Taco ~$15–20/person  ·  Shopping: your wallet, your rules
7:30 PM

Sushi Night

We clean up, dress up, and go out for a gorgeous sushi dinner. Think candlelit, beautiful, the kind of place that ends up all over your camera roll.

~$60–80/person

The Main Event

🎸
10:00 AM

Matching navy athletic sets — yes, we coordinate. We hit the courts right at our apartment for a friendly but competitive round-robin tournament. May the best cowgirl win.

Included with apartment

Lunch at Home

Refuel back at the house with snacks, leftovers, and plenty of water before the big night ahead. Rest those legs — you'll need them on Broadway.

Dinner at White Duck Taco

Fuel up before the big night out. White Duck Taco's creative, bold tacos — the perfect pre-Broadway meal to get everyone fired up and ready to hit the honky-tonks.

Party on Broadway / Music City 🎶
